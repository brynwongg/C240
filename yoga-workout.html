Yoga Routine

Stretch, relax, and restore your body with these calming yoga flows perfect for students.

Morning Yoga Flow (10–15 mins)

  • Cat-Cow stretches – 1 min
  • Sun Salutation – 3 rounds
  • Warrior I & II – 30 sec each side
  • Seated Forward Fold – 1 min

Post-Workout Cooldown

  • Downward Dog – 30 sec
  • Pigeon Pose – 1 min each leg
  • Child's Pose – 1 min
  • Supine Twist – 1 min each side

Evening Relaxation (Bedtime)

  • Legs-up-the-wall – 2–3 min
  • Reclined Butterfly – 2 min
  • Breathwork – 5 slow deep breaths
  • Savasana – 3–5 min
