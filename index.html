🏋️ Fitness for Students

Build habits that support your body and mind. Plan your fitness goals using the calendar and explore workouts tailored to a student lifestyle.

⚡ Quick Start Guide

  • ✅ Beginner-friendly: Start with 15–20 mins, 3x a week
  • ⏱️ Busy day? Squeeze in 5–10 minute workouts
  • 🎯 Choose your goal: Cardio, Strength, or Flexibility

💪 Workout Categories

🏃 Cardio

  • Jump rope (5–10 mins)
  • Jog or walk around school
  • 10-min YouTube dance workouts

💪 Strength

  • Push-ups, squats, planks
  • Backpack or bottle curls
  • Resistance band circuits

🧘 Flexibility

  • Beginner yoga flow
  • Foam rolling or stretching
  • Sun salutations (3 rounds)
