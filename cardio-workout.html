Cardio Workout

Improve your stamina and burn calories with these student-friendly cardio routines.

Indoor Options

  • Treadmill – 20 minutes at moderate pace
  • HIIT – 30 sec sprint, 30 sec walk (10 rounds)
  • Stair climbing – 5 rounds of 2 floors
  • Jump rope – 3 sets of 1 min each

Outdoor Activities

  • Jog around campus – 2 to 3 km
  • Brisk walk – 30 minutes
  • Cycling – 20 to 30 minutes
  • Sports – Basketball, football, or frisbee

Low Impact Cardio

  • Walking in place – 15 minutes
  • Beginner dance workouts – YouTube
  • Elliptical or rowing machine – 10–15 mins
